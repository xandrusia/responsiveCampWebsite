Camp description
Enjoy an unforgettable winter in the northern slopes of Rila mountain, Borovets, Bulgaria in one of the most beautiful places in the world, you`ll be able to learn new things, make friends from every corner of the world and of course, have lots of fun. Everyone aged 10 to 15 years can participate in the camp for one, two or three weeks, with or without an overnight stay. Choose between two options:
→ Morning: English or coding lessons | Afternoon: Skiing or snowboarding
→ All day: Skiing or Snowboarding During the evenings and weekends.

Fees
→ CHF 1500 (per 8 days, one student only, accomodation with 3 meals per day)
→ CHF 1200 Ski pass ( per 8 days, ski pass for 8 hours)
→ CHF 600 (round-trip plane ticket, economic class)
→ Pocket money (at your own discretion)

Activities
→ Snow games
→ Sledding
→ Skiing/Snowboarding
→ Hikes
→ Ice skating
